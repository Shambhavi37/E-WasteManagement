E-Kachra.com

We at e-kachra are a team of passionate youths who aim to serve this planet better by reducing substantially the amount of e-waste being generated.

LEARN MORE Web Resources

You will never consider any sleek designed laptop or smartphone as waste.

E-Waste is on the verge of becoming the greatest problem the world has ever faced. Any electronic device that has become unwanted, non-working or obsolete is categorized as e-waste.
With the technology advancing at such a high rate, many electronic devices become “Trash” after a few short years of use. 

We generate about 40 million tons of e-waste every year.

  • Collection
  • Sorting
  • Processing

Collection

We will have collection centers for every city
where individuals, kabadiwallas & scrap-dealers
can sell their e-waste.

Waste to Wealth

We can fix an evaluation category for most types of e-waste. When a person will bring
his/her electronic waste, it will be evaluated and he/she will be paid accordingly

Shipment

Once the collected waste has been
segregated, it will be shipped to the
Central Recycling Facility (CRF)
for further processing.

Sorting

As the initial step, we will sort out the
repairable and non-repairable appliances
at the collection center itself.

Dismantling

The first step which will help in further sorting
of the e-waste , will be dismantling. In this
process, we extract all the useful parts which can be reused again

Recycling

Once we are done with the  sorting 
and dismantling processes, we can send the e-waste to the CRF for recycling.

Central Recycling Facility 
(CRF)

The collected e-waste will be shipped to the
CRF from the Collection Centers where
it will be segregated again, shredded and 
recycled into different products.

Sorting & Processing

The waste devices will be dismantled and shredded into pieces less than 100 mm in size.
Then it will undergo various separation methods like hydrometallurgy and electrochemical recycling.

Product

The metal ore procured after processing 
can be sent for refining and used whereas the plastic can be crushed and glued to foem decorative items, furniture and others.
